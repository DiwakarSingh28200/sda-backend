1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

SDA shall offer Roadside Assistance and related services as specified in Annexure 1. These services may be updated or modified by SDA, in consultation with the Dealer, through written acceptance email or official correspondence. Such modifications shall become binding only upon written acceptance by the Dealer.

The Dealer shall not, under any circumstances, solicit, negotiate, or accept business or issue documents in SDA's name without express prior written authorization. Any additional offerings or representations to end customers must be listed, mutually agreed upon, and configured in the SDA platform.

2. DEALER'S RESPONSIBILITIES
  • The Dealer shall be solely responsible for marketing and selling SDA's RSA Subscription Plan to its end customers.
  • The Dealer shall collect payments from the end customers for the RSA services offered and ensure that customers receive their RSA service certificates digitally, as prescribed by SDA.
  • The Dealer shall ensure that all payments collected are settled with SDA in accordance with Clause 3.
  • The Dealer shall not be responsible for delivering the RSA services described in the Subscription Plan or addressing claims relating to those services.
3. PAYMENT TERMS
  • The Dealer shall collect payment from the customer at the time of RSA sale and remit the due amount to SDA within seven (7) business days via bank transfer or Dealer's wallet (bank details in Annexure 2).
  • SDA shall raise GST-compliant tax invoices on a fortnightly, as mutually agreed, based on actual RSA sales.
  • Dealer shall ensure its GST registration remains valid and active throughout the Term of this Agreement. SDA shall not be liable for any input tax credit denial arising from Dealer's non-compliance.
  • Delayed payments beyond ten (10) business days shall attract interest at 18% p.a., calculated daily until payment is received.
  • During the initial onboarding, SDA may provide a credit limit to the Dealer's wallet, valid for 30 days. After expiry, the Dealer must make advance payments to continue RSA issuance. Unused credit shall lapse post- validity.
  • Policy issuance will be suspended if the wallet lacks funds or if payments are overdue.
  • In case of non-payment beyond forty-five (45) days from the due date, SDA reserves the right to cancel the corresponding RSA Subscription Plan issued under the Dealer's account, without further notice.
4. COMMISSION AND INCENTIVES
  • SDA shall credit to the Dealer a commission for RSA Subscription Plan sold, as per commission structure defined in Annexure 3 including slab-wise earnings, volume-based bonuses, and seasonal incentives.
  • Such commissions shall be credited on a daily basis to the Dealer's wallet maintained with SDA.
  • The Dealer shall raise a monthly invoice or relevant documentation for such fees for compliance with applicable tax laws.
  • SDA's liability toward the Dealer shall be limited to the commission payable under this Agreement.
  • SDA reserves the right to revise commission rates with a 30-day prior notice, applicable prospectively.
5. SECURITY DEPOSIT
  • The Dealer has provided SDA with a post-dated security deposit cheque, details of which are acknowledged and recorded separately in Annexure 2.
  • The Dealer agrees that encashment of the security cheque shall not be construed as a penalty, but as compensation for non-performance under this Agreement.
  • SDA reserves the right to encash the security cheque in the event of default or breach of payment obligations by the Dealer under this Agreement. Dealer shall be notified at least 5 business days prior to encashment of the security cheque due to default.
6. TERM

This Agreement shall be valid for a period of three (3) years from the Effective Date, unless earlier terminated in accordance with Clause 7.

The Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms, unless either Party provides a 30- day written notice of non-renewal.

Either Party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice without assigning any reason.

  • There is a change in the Dealer's corporate structure;

  • A receiver or similar officer is appointed for the Dealer's assets.
